Our Resorts

KAWANA

The Hillside Sanctuary
東京から約1時間半。丘の上に佇む、約30,000㎡の広大なリゾート。敷地内から湧き出る源泉温泉、心身を解放するウェルネスプログラム。高台から美しい海と名門ゴルフコースを眺める、非日常の楽園。

The Hillside Sanctuary
Just 1.5 hours from Tokyo, the KAWANA PROJECT, will be a highly exclusive resort spanning 30,000 square meters and nestled atop a hill with an unparalleled view. The on-site natural hot spring will rejuvenate your senses, while exclusive wellness programs uplift your body and mind. Gaze upon the breathtaking vista of the ocean and one of the most prestigious golf courses in the world, transforming your stay into an extraordinary paradise.

View More

LAKE TOYA

Sense of time
11万年前の噴火により生まれた洞爺湖。地球が永き時をかけて作り出した幻想的な景色を眺めながら、心身を整える。静寂の中、「時」を感じる神秘のリゾート。

Passage of Time
Originally formed by a volcanic eruption 110,000 years ago, Lake Toya offers breathtaking scenery crafted by the hand of nature over the course of millennia. Here, you can enjoy the passage of time in a haven of serenity. Rejuvenate your body and mind at HERM’s mystical resort at Lake Toya.

View More

SOWAKA

京都・祇園。100年以上もの歴史ある老舗料亭をリノベーションし、古と新が入り混じる、23室のスモールラグジュアリーホテル。日本の繊細な美に触れ、奥深い京都の魅力に没入する体験を。

Renovated from a ryotei (traditional Japanese restaurant) with over 100 years of history, SOWAKA is a 23-room small luxury hotel where the old meets the new. Experience the subtle beauty of Japan and immerse yourself in the deep charm of Kyoto.
